‘Oh, I had almost thought that!" exclaimed Hamlet. ‘My uncle!’

‘Yes, that incestuous, that adulterous, beast. With the witchcraft of his intelligence, with his traitorous
qualities - oh evil intelligence and qualities that have the power to seduce like that - he forced the
will of my most seeming-virtuous queen to his shameful lust. Oh Hamlet, what a falling-off was
there!’
